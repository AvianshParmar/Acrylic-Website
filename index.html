About Us

Who We Are

Mukesh Enterprise is a leading acrylic manufacturer based in Jamnagar, Gujarat. Since 2019, we've produced top-quality acrylic rods, awards, trophies, and customized mementos. With a skilled team and advanced machinery, we deliver creative, durable, and visually striking acrylic products to businesses, schools, NGO's and corporates.

Why Choose Us

With a monthly capacity of 3,000+ units, Mukesh Enterprise ensures timely delivery, consistent quality, and complete client satisfaction. Here's why our customers trust us:

  • Custom-made acrylic trophies and rods
  • Fast production with tight quality control
  • Transparent, ethical business values
  • Nationwide and international delivery

Our Products

Acrylic Rods
Durable, crystal-clear rods ideal for displays, signage & industrial needs. Our acrylic rods are precision-cast, UV-resistant, and available in multiple diameters. Perfect for lighting fixtures, lab equipment, decorative columns, and more.
Custom Acrylic Trophy
Elegant acrylic trophies for professional recognition and branding. Designed for corporate events and competitions, our trophies offer premium finish, laser-cut precision, and complete personalization with names, logos, and shapes.
Acrylic Mementos
Elegant mementos for celebrations, academic, or professional milestones. With smooth polish and clear edges, these mementos reflect lasting impressions. Fully customizable with your logo, occasion name, and design of your choice.
